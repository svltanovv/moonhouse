Questions

Frequently asked questions

What timeframe is required for a property purchase abroad?
Timing depends on the country and complexity of the transaction, but it usually takes a few months from initial selection to completion.
What factors influence the choice of property location abroad?
Location factors include availability of infrastructure, proximity to the sea or mountains, tax policies of the region, rental demand and long-term capital growth potential.
What costs are associated with owning property abroad?
In addition to the purchase price, there may be costs for taxes, utilities, property management, maintenance and repairs. Our specialists will provide a full cost breakdown for each country.
How to assess the rental yield potential of property abroad?
Rental yields depend on location, property type and market conditions. Analysing comparable rental rates will help to assess the potential return on investment.
How to ensure the safety of property investments abroad?
It is important to conduct detailed market research, co-operate with reliable agents and lawyers, and keep abreast of changes in local legislation. We provide full legal support at every stage.
What documents are required to buy property abroad?
Documents and requirements vary by country, but usually include a valid passport and proof of funds. In some countries a visa or residence permit may be required.
