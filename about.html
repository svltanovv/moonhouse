Our story

International real estate since 1998

Moonhouse Investments Limited is a private limited company with a long track record in international real estate. We operate offices in Dubai, New York, Astana and Singapore.

The company mission is to teach potential investors to earn money by investing in the international real estate market. We provide complete support — from property selection to legal registration and asset management.

Our approach: individual solutions for each client, transactional transparency, guaranteed confidentiality, and no service fees charged to buyers.
